Computer Science Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering is one of the most dynamic and in-demand branches of engineering.

CSE focuses on computer systems, programming, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and software development. Students gain strong foundations in algorithms, data structures, and emerging technologies to design and build innovative solutions for real-world problems.

Electrical Engineering (EE)

Electrical Engineering powers the modern world..

EE focuses on electricity, power generation, transmission, and distribution systems. It also covers electronics, control systems, and renewable energy technologies. Electrical engineers work on projects ranging from small electronic devices to large-scale power grids.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

ECE bridges hardware and communication systems.

This branch combines electronics, embedded systems, and telecommunication technologies. Students learn about circuits, digital systems, VLSI, wireless communication, and IoT. ECE graduates contribute to innovations in mobile networks, satellites, consumer electronics, and automation.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is the backbone of infrastructure development.

This branch deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of physical structures like roads, bridges, dams, and buildings. Civil engineers ensure safety, sustainability, and efficiency in infrastructure projects, playing a vital role in urban and rural development.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is Where Motion Meets Mastery.

Mechanical engineering is a broad and foundational field of engineering that applies principles of physics, mathematics, and materials science to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems and devices that involve movement and force

Fashion Designing

Fashion Designing is where creativity meets lifestyle.

This branch focuses on clothing, textiles, and design principles. Students learn garment construction, fashion illustration, trends, and merchandising. The program nurtures creativity and equips learners to work in fashion houses, textile industries, or as independent designers.
